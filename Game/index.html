Ludo Neo Rules

  • Deployment: A player must roll a 6 to move a token out of the base onto the starting cell.
  • Extra Rolls: Rolling a 6, capturing an opponent's piece, or landing a token in the Home triangle grants an additional roll.
  • Three Sixes Rule: Rolling three consecutive 6s skips the turn to prevent lucky streaks.
  • Safe Zones (★): Star cells and starting cells are safe zones. Opponents cannot capture you there, and multiple players can occupy safe cells.
  • Capture: Landing on an opponent's token in a non-safe cell captures it, sending it back to their base.
  • Home Stretch: Once a token circles the board, it enters the home stretch. A player needs an exact roll to land a piece in the central Home triangle.
